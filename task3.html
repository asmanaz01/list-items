TASK THREE

Recipe: Biryani

Ingredients

  • Rice
    • Soak for 30 minutes before cooking
  • Chicken
    • Marinate for 1 hour
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice
  2. Marinate the chicken
  3. Fry onions till golden brown
  4. Add chicken and spices
  5. Add rice and cook together
